# Simple Earn

Simple Earn is a service for generating yield by depositing USDC into verified vaults, without needing to understand complex DeFi farming strategies.

* **Curated vault strategies.** DeGate curates stablecoin yield opportunities on major blockchain ecosystems, selected for stable, lower-risk profiles.
* **Multi-chain USDC deposits.** You can deposit USDC from any supported chain instantly. The system manages your balances across networks, removing the need for manual bridging.
* **Simplified yield management.** From the initial deposit to claiming rewards, the dashboard lets you track and manage earnings at a glance.

_Example: you can use USDC from any chain and open a Simple Earn position in a few clicks._
