# Multi-chain USDC Earn

Earn **flexible 4.1% APY on USDC**, with rewards **automatically distributed to users daily** and compounded seamlessly. You can deposit or withdraw anytime while your funds continue earning. Rates are subject to change based on market conditions and campaigns.

Supported chains for USDC currently includes Base, Solana, BSC, Optimism, Arbitrum, Aptos, WorldCoin, Avalanche, Ethereum, Polygon, Sonic.&#x20;
