# Turbo Range

Turbo Range enables users to deposit xStocks pairs into AMM liquidity pools with a single click, maximizing capital efficiency through a simplified interface.

* Raydium Pool Integration: DeGate serves as a streamlined front-end terminal for Raydium’s pools. Users can access deep liquidity directly within the DeGate interface without navigating to external platforms.
* Multi-chain USDC Deposits: By leveraging DeGate’s multi-chain system, you can deposit USDC from any supported chain instantly. The system manages your balances across networks, removing the need for manual bridging.\
  \
  &#xNAN;_&#x45;xample: You can use USDC (from any chain) and open a Turbo Range position with only a few clicks._
