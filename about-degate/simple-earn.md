# Simple Earn

Simple Earn is a user-friendly service designed for those who want to generate yield by depositing USDC into verified vaults without the need to understand complex DeFi farming strategies.

* **Curated Vault Strategies:** We curate low-risk yet stable yield stablecoin opportunities on major blockchain ecosystems.
* **Multi-chain USDC Deposits:** By leveraging DeGate’s multi-chain system, you can deposit USDC from any supported chain instantly. The system manages your balances across networks, removing the need for manual bridging.
* **Simplified Yield Management:** From initial deposit to claiming rewards, the intuitive dashboard allows you to track and manage your earnings at a glance.

_Example: You can use USDC (from any chain) and open a Simple Earn position with only a few clicks._
